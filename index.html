How AURUM thinks

Built on a confluence-based Pine Script that scores 14 separate systems and emits BUY / SELL only when enough of them agree.

01

Trend & momentum

EMA200 trend filter, Supertrend direction and flips, ADX trend / chop gating, fast-RSI confirmation and volume surge.

02

SMC structure

Swing-high / swing-low tracking, BOS and CHoCH detection, displacement candles, premium / discount zone bias.

03

ICT & liquidity

Equal-high / equal-low sweep liquidity, FVG and order-block confluence, inducement traps, AMD-aware smart SL.

04

Flow & oscillators

VCO oscillator, RSI / CCI / Stoch / MFI consensus, VWAP relative position, CVD direction, volume profile POC / VAH / VAL.

05

Tiered scoring

Score in [-25, +25]. SNIPER ≥ ±15, HIGH ≥ ±10, STD above threshold. Momentum override for strong directional moves.

06

Risk model

Smart SL anchored to last swing, tiered TP1 / TP2 / TP3, fixed-points or ATR-based, every output paired with R:R math.

Order-flow / footprint is the next frontier — it requires paid CME tape data and is on the roadmap, not in the demo.
